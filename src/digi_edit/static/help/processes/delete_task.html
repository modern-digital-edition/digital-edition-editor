Deleting a TaskÂ¶

To delete a Task, either click on the icon in the Task list or on the Task page. You will be asked to confirm that you wish to delete the Task, as this process cannot be undone. If you confirm the deletion, then the Task will immediately be deleted, its copy will be deleted, and the backend will also be synchronised immediately.
